Game Rules of Snakes and Ladders

The game of Snakes and Ladders is played between two or more players on a board having numbered squares. The objective is to navigate the player's token from start to finish by rolling dice.

If you land on a ladder, you move up to a higher number. If you land on a snake, you slide down to a lower number.
